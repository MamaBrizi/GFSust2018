Innovative Mobility Hub Seeks to Reshape Our Cities

Uber Technologies, Inc. | Various Locations

With a vision to improve communities and reshape our cities, Gannett Fleming responded to the Uber Elevate Skyport Challenge with an inspiring and powerful concept. The SKYPORT is an entirely new urban intermodal hub offering an easy-to-navigate space for passengers to access vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL) aircraft for their daily commutes. The design concept maximizes passenger comfort and safety while minimizing environmental impacts. Modular and highly flexible, SKYPORT efficiently links all forms of urban transportation, making cities simpler, quieter, and smarter.

Our approach to the SKYPORT design centers around the PAWâ€”an optimized configuration for landings, takeoffs, drone charging, and passenger comfort. The modularity of the design offers flexibility by allowing a PAW to be replicated as many times as needed for the size of the space and anticipated demand. It can be built atop or between existing buildings to create convenient air transit routes across cities large and small.

The design concept improves our communities by integrating environmentally friendly features, such as renewable energy, sustainable materials, and daylighting, while providing an experience that is intuitive, inviting, and safe.

Sustainable Features & Outcomes:

  • Incorporates photovoltaic receptors for solar recharging.
  • Uses batteries that can be charged from grid or solar power to recharge the drones, reducing environmental impacts.
  • Living wall and ample use of vegetation throughout the space.
  • Prominent use of daylighting techniques to reduce artificial lighting demand.
  • Includes sound walls to minimize noise migration during takeoff and landing.
